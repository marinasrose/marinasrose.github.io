About Me

Hi, I'm Marina!

I've been crocheting for 11 years. When I've told people that I crochet, I've had a lot of people tell me they want to learn. After talking to various people over the years, I decided that I want to create a website to teach others the art of crochet.

I've been married for 13 years to my highschool sweetheart and we have six kids. I've been belly dancing for 4.5 years and love it. I enjoy reading, singing, and watching movies with my husband.
